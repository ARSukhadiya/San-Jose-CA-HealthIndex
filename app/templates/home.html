Health Prediction App

Our Neighborhood Health Prediction App is designed to help communities understand health trends and make informed decisions. Leveraging data analytics, machine learning, and visualization, this tool empowers users to explore health factors and predict health outcomes in various neighborhoods.

Project Overview

The Neighborhood Health Prediction App uses publicly available health and socioeconomic data to analyze and predict neighborhood health outcomes. By examining factors like household income, education, and health statistics, users can explore correlations and gain insights to improve public health strategies.

Key Features

Data Visualization

Interactive graphs and charts to explore neighborhood health trends and correlations.

Health Prediction Model

Predict health outcomes based on various socioeconomic and health factors.

User-Friendly Interface

A simple, intuitive interface that guides users through the analysis and prediction processes.

Uses and Applications

  • Public health professionals can use the tool to identify high-risk neighborhoods and allocate resources efficiently.
  • Community organizations can analyze neighborhood health trends to plan health-focused community programs.
  • Researchers can explore data correlations to support health-related studies and policy recommendations.
